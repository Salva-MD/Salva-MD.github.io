I developed a binary classification model to help Beta Bank identify customers at high risk of contract cancellation. Since retaining customers is more cost-effective than acquiring new ones, the solution aimed to predict churn and optimize retention strategies.

Objective: Maximize the F1-Score (minimum 0.59) and evaluate overall model performance with AUC-ROC.

Stack: Python, pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn, scikit-learn

Results: The best model achieved an F1-Score of 0.62 and an AUC-ROC of 0.86, exceeding the bank’s required threshold.
